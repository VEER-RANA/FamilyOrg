# FamilyOrg User Manual (Simple Step-by-Step)

This guide explains the app in the same order you use it: **top to bottom, page by page**.

---

## 1) App Flow (Quick Map)

Use FamilyOrg in this order:

1. **Login / Register**
2. **Dashboard**
3. **Open an Event or Trip card**
4. **Use page buttons (Invite, Status, Theme, Edit, etc.)**
5. **Manage tasks, budget, comments, photos**
6. **Check Notifications**
7. **Update Profile if needed**

## 8.1) Google Photos Album Link (Step-by-Step)

Use this flow to share photos with everyone:

1. If you are **Organizer**, go to the Event/Trip details page and open the **Photos** section.
2. Click **Open Google Photos** (or open Google Photos manually), then create a new album.
3. After album creation, click **Share** in Google Photos and copy the **public share link**.
4. Come back to FamilyOrg ‚Üí **Photos** section ‚Üí **Add Link** box, then paste that shared link and save.
5. All Organizers and Participants can see this link and open the album.

### View Album behavior
- Clicking **View Album** redirects members to Google Photos.
- Members can view photos, and they can add/download images based on Google Photos share permissions.

## 9) Invitations (How to Use)

### As Organizer
1. Open Event/Trip details.
2. Click **Invite**.
3. Select member.
4. Select role:
   - Organizer
   - Participant
5. Optional message.
6. Click **Send Invitation**.

<div style="page-break-before: always;"></div>

### As Invitee
1. Open Dashboard ‚Üí Pending Invitations.
2. Click:
   - **Accept** ‚Üí you join item.
   - **Decline** ‚Üí choose reason + optional custom message.

### Decline reasons available
- Schedule Conflict
- Not Interested
- Already Committed
- Too Far
- Budget Constraints
- Other

### Organizer visibility
- Organizer can open **Invitation Status** and see:
  - Pending/Accepted/Declined
  - Decline reasons (only organizer can see)

## 12) Roles (Simple)

Only 2 roles are used:

- **Organizer**
  - Full control
